Introduction Online Payment Made Simple

  • WalaoPay provides a means for your members and customers to pay you via our automated bank deposits. One simple integration gives you access to accept deposits and payments via all major banks.
  • The WalaoPay Payment Gateway enables merchants to receive money from their customers by temporarily redirecting them to our payment servers. After the payment is complete, the customer is returned to the merchant’s site and seconds later the merchant receives notification about the payment along with the transaction’s details.
